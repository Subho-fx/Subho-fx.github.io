About Me

I am a digital FX artist and a 3D modeler with a broad understanding of basic graphic design. My main forte lies in creating artificial dynamic movements of 3D objects for various effects in motion pictures. I use my skills in 3D modeling and dynamics to narrate my story and give it a cinematic effect.

My core skills lie in working on Houdini for dynamics, Autodesk Maya and 3ds Max for 3D modeling, and miscellaneous work and few other Softwares like Nuke and Adobe Aftereffect for compositing. I am also a trained graphic designer with years of experience in the industry.
